10-Stages of Water Purification Process

1. Sand Filtration

We start by removing visible impurities (like dirt, rust, and silt) using high-grade sand media filtration. This stage lays the foundation for clear water.

2. Activated Carbon Filtration

Water then passes through high-grade **Activated Carbon** to remove chlorine, harmful organic compounds, pesticides, bad odour, and colour, dramatically improving taste and odour.

3. Micron Filtration

Through a multi-stage **Micron Filtration** system (10, 5, and 0.5 microns), even the tiniest particles and suspended solids remaining are trapped, ensuring crystal clarity before the next stage.

4. Reverse Osmosis (RO)

This is where deep purification happens. The water is forced through a semi-permeable membrane to remove dissolved salts (**TDS**), heavy metals (like lead, arsenic), viruses, and bacteria, making the water incredibly pure and safe for daily hydration.

5. UV Sterilization

Water purity is increased by **UV light** that neutralizes bacteria, viruses, and other pathogens by disrupting their DNA structure. This acts as a reliable final safeguard against germs.

6. Ozonation

Ozone – a natural, eco-friendly disinfectant – is bubbled through the water. It ensures complete disinfection, leaving the water fresh, light, and long-lasting.

7. Silver Ionization

We use modern **Silver Ionization technology** to prevent any potential post-treatment bacterial regrowth and ensure the water remains microbiologically safe until the moment of distribution.

8. TDS & PH Balancing

After purification, we fine-tune the TDS and pH levels to meet the highest standards, ensuring the water contains essential minerals and is easily absorbed by your body.

9. Stainless Steel Storage

Purified water is stored in **food-grade SS tanks** (Stainless Steel) under controlled temperature and hygienic conditions to prevent contamination before bottling.

10. Automated Bottling

In our fully automated facility, the water is bottled into sterilized containers using **touchless technology** and sealed securely, ensuring the water is pure and delivered "without ever being touched by hand."
